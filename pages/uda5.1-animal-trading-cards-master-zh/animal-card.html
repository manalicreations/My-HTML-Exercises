  • Name: Zebra
  • Class: Mammals
  • Size: About 1.2–1.3m at shoulder & 2–2.6m long
  • Lifspan: 25-30 years

Zebra is evolved from original horse in 4 million years ago, and it has never been domesticated livestock. There are three species of zebras: the plains zebra, the Grévy's zebra and the mountain zebra. Male Zebre live alone, while the female, young and elder live together. Zebra are herbivorous animals, their favour food might be grass, shrubs, twigs, leaves and bark. Predators like Lion,Leopard,Wild dog etc. are Zebra's natural enemise.
